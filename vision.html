Our Vision

Taste of best source



> We believe in bringing raw authentic products directly from farmers for you.

> Our aim is to establish a relationship between farmer produce and your health.

> Unadulterated, Un-processed and Raw procurement is our first choice.

> Our company's objective is to promote products derived from Himalayan or sub-Himalayan zones.

> Removing middleman, implementation of modern technology and rise of agricultural produce in hilly areas is our aim.

> We promote sustainability & environment friendly agricultural practices.

> Providing a good value to hill farmers for there produce and providing stability to them, that's what we look to do.

> We are a customer friendly start-up and listen to the demands of customers, hence variability in our product line-up is common.
